Bridge Between STATIC COOLDOWN: 50 / 40 / 30 / 20 (BASED ON LEVEL)


INNATE - AWAKENED SPIRIT: Udyr has no ultimate ability and instead has 4 basic abilities that each incur a Cooldown icon 1.5-second global cooldown when cast.
